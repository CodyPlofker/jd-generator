Phase 1: Research
Outersignal Personas
Begin with output of angles and insights

Research Activities

  • Push segments to Klaviyo → email persona surveys
  • Analyze reviews for insider language & "golden nugget" copy
  • Reddit/FB group research for psychographics
  • Listenlabs survey for deeper understanding
  • Create IG account & interact as the persona
  • Scan FB Ads Library for non-competitive success
  • TikTok trends data for key topics
Output: 1-Pager / Persona Brief

Persona Brief Contains

  • Who they are
  • Professions
  • Demographics
  • Psychographics
  • Key motivations & desires
  • Why our brand appeals
  • 4-6 core value props to test
  • Creator profiles they follow
  • Creators that fit this persona
  • Successful non-competitive ads
  • Golden nugget reviews
  • Sample customer profiles
Goal: Deep understanding of persona before any creative spend. Build the brief that guides all creative and media decisions.

Phase 3: Scale, Iterate, or Cut

Green = Scale

  • Scale winners within ad set
  • Expand to other campaigns
  • Produce 2nd batch of statics
  • Create sub-persona batches
  • Animate to GIFs/short video
  • Test awareness levels
  • Test unique mechanisms
  • Graduate to Phase 4

Yellow = Iterate

  • Refine angle/messaging
  • Test new creative formats
  • Adjust targeting
  • Re-test with variations
  • Consider sub-segments

Red = Cut

  • Cut losses
  • Move on from concept
  • Document learnings
  • Apply insights to other personas
If Green → Continue to Phase 4
Goal: Make clear go/no-go decisions. Only invest production resources in validated winners. Cut losers fast, iterate on maybes.
